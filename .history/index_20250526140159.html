A social media platform uniting creators who produce high-quality, intellectual, and value-driven content with innovative filtering and multi-monetization models.

Our Vision

Twinkle aims to filter the media industry and create a new market for the creator economy and business. We're building a platform that values quality over quantity, depth over virality, and sustainable creator economics over exploitative algorithms.

Our Mission

To empower content creators in Uzbekistan and beyond with tools, community, and monetization opportunities that enable them to build sustainable careers doing what they love. We strive to elevate the quality of media content while providing creators with the resources they need to succeed.

Key Differentiators

  • Quality-focused content filtering and recommendation algorithms
  • Multiple monetization streams for creators (subscriptions, tips, marketplace, sponsorships)
  • Built-in educational resources and creator development programs
  • Focus on intellectual and educational content rather than purely entertainment
  • Regional expertise and connections in Central Asian markets

The Future of Content Monetization in Emerging Markets

Our CEO Behruz Sayfiddinov recently shared his insights on how content creators in emerging markets can leverage new monetization strategies to build sustainable careers. In his analysis, Behruz highlights the unique challenges faced by creators in regions like Central Asia, including limited advertising markets, payment processing difficulties, and audience spending power. However, he also points to innovative solutions that Twinkle is developing, such as micro-patronage models, cross-border payment solutions, and hybrid monetization approaches that combine subscription, one-time purchases, and sponsored content. Building a Quality-First Content Platform

In our latest blog post, CTO Alisher Zokirovich discusses the technical challenges of building a platform that prioritizes quality content while maintaining scalability.
